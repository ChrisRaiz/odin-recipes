Summer Grilled Pork Chops

A porkchop perfect for the summer time!

This porkchop recipe is perfect for the summertime, paired with fruits and a refreshing drink this will brighten even the brightest of days!

Just a few ingredients and by following these easy steps you'll have a beautifully cooked porkchop in no time!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 cups honey
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 4 center-cut pork chops, 1/2-inch thick
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
